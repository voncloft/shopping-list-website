Steps:
Preperation:
1). Set over to 425

Meat:
1). Add olive oil to skillet over medium add sausage
2). Cook for 8-10 minutes breaking it up
3). Make a well in the center put garlic in and cook for a minute
4) add Marinara sauce and italian seasoning and simmer for 2-3 mins

Instructions:
1). Put one layer of Cauliflower on bottom of 9x13 dish
2). Dollop half ricotta over it
3). Sprinkle half of shredded Parmesan
4). Top with half of sausage Marina
5). Sprinkle half shredded Mozzarella.
6). Repeat until done
7). Cook for 10-15 minutes
