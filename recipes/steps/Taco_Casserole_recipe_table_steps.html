Steps:
1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees

2) In large pan heat olive oil over medium-high head add ground beef, break apart cook for 10 minutes

3) add water and taco seasoning bring to boil, then simmer for 2-5 mins

4) ransfer the beef to a large baking dish. Add the diced peppers, onions, and drained diced tomatoes with green chiles. Mix everything together. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.

5) Bake for 20-30 minutes

6) Remove from the oven and top the casserole with any toppings you like, such as lettuce, tomatoes, avocados and/or cilantro.
