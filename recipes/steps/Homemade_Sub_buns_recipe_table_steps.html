Steps:
1) In a large bowl, mix warm water, yeast, sugar, and oil.
2) Add salt and most of the flour.
3) Mix until it becomes a dough.
4) Knead about 8 to 10 minutes until smooth.
5) Cover and let rise about 1 hour, until doubled.
6) Divide into 14 equal pieces.
7) Shape each into a 12-inch loaf.
8) Put them on parchment-lined trays.
9) Cover and let rise again 30 to 45 minutes.
10) Bake at 375°F for about 18 to 22 minutes.
11) Cool fully before bagging.
